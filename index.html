GLP-1 Leadership Opportunity

People can find GLP-1 online. What they can’t get everywhere is smart supervision.

CKM should own the local message that medication alone is not the same as a medical weight-loss plan. The best outcomes come from candidacy screening, dose guidance, side-effect management, nutrition support, hydration, protein strategy, and body-composition monitoring.

Why patients choose supervised care

  • More thoughtful screening and treatment selection
  • Support during dose escalation
  • Help with nausea, constipation, and plateaus
  • Fit3D tracking to protect lean-mass progress

Education topics CKM should own

  • Semaglutide vs tirzepatide
  • Best practices for hydration and protein intake
  • How to preserve muscle while losing weight
  • How long treatment may last and how maintenance works
