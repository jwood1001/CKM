Provider profile

A medical background grounded in care, access, and real relationships.

Chelsie Mangum is an Advanced Practice Registered Nurse with experience in primary care, women’s health, and emergency nursing. CKM Health & Wellness was built around a simple idea: patients deserve more time, more clarity, and a provider who treats them like a whole person.

Care philosophy

The strongest version of CKM is not a high-volume clinic. It is a boutique medical practice where patients feel listened to, guided well, and supported with practical next steps.

That philosophy matters across every service line. In primary care, it means more context and better continuity. In weight loss, it means supervision and strategy rather than convenience alone. In aesthetic care, it means thoughtful planning and natural-looking outcomes.

What patients should feel

  • I am being listened to.
  • I have a real plan, not a generic one.
  • I know what the next step is.
  • I can come back here for guidance, not just a transaction.
