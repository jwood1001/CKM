Provider profile

A medical background grounded in time, attention, and honest conversation.

Hello, I'm Chelsie Mangum, an Advanced Practice Registered Nurse Practitioner (APRN) with 8 years of experience in primary care. I previously worked in women's health as an APRN and as a registered nurse in the emergency department. I obtained my Master of Nursing from the University of South Alabama and a Bachelor of Psychology from the University of Central Florida.

Why CKM Health and Wellness exists

CKM exists to give patients more time with their provider — enough time to actually talk through what is going on, ask the questions they came in with, and leave with a clear plan rather than a rushed referral.

Whether the concern is weight, preventive care, cosmetics, or something harder to name, the approach is the same: listen first, understand the full picture, and give you a next step that actually makes sense for your situation.

No insurance is needed to be seen. Patients are encouraged to contact the clinic by phone or email to schedule an appointment or ask questions about concerns they would like to discuss.

What you can expect here

  • I am being listened to.
  • I have a real plan, not a generic one.
  • I know what the next step is.
  • I can come back here for guidance, not just a transaction.
